## 08:35 Saturday 16 September 2017 JST

Arms are sore after rafting yesterday, but generally had a great
time.  Got dumped a couple of times, and banged up my knees a bit on
rocks, but I was wearing a helmet, so no worries.

I am thankful my phone is waterproof!
