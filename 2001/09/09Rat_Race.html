1:12pm CDT Sunday 9 September 2001

Saw Rat Race with Tina today (free tix cause she works at the theater). It started out not entirely funny, but they pulled all the threads together in a pretty funny way in the end. Plus a couple good plot twists, and some wacked out betting humor.

I was reminded of a book (The Blue Adept???) that described a futuristic society where the rich bet on everything. The rich guys in this movie did too. And they bet on some gross/wack things.

My favorite favorite scene was the helicopter scene.

Oh, and the jeep trying to pull over the radar was great.

Good enough to see at a matinee, but not full price.
