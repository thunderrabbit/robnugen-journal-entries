Monsters, Inc was *brilliant*!!!

You absolutely must see this movie because (in no particular order):

  • It was funny
  • It was fully computer animated
  • The writing was brilliant
  • All of the above reasons again

I totally loved this film. I thought all the good stories were written, and this one comes along and produces a whole new setting, ready for a million new stories to come out of it.

The chase scene near the end of the movie was great roller coastery fun. Go see it!

Oh! And, speaking of new stories, Star Wars Episode II, Attack of the Clones trailer appeared before Monsters, Inc. The entire soundtrack of the trailer was Darth Vader's breath while we watched varied and random scenes. Then we hear vwack vwack of two light sabers and that was it. More movie brilliance.
