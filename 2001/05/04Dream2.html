8:40 am Friday 4 May 2001

dream

Downtown skating, and tomorrow was the last day athat Diane would be in town so she and I could go skating together. I wanted to call her to confirm a skate date with her.

I crossed the street because I saw a very short rollercoaster - a short version of Greezed Lightning (a shuttle loop coaster for those who know what that means) that was built between a building and the street surrounded by a wobbly chain link fence.

I leaned on the fence and asked the guy how much it cost to ride and he said it was free once I get inside the fence, and that normally costs ten dollars. But if I just climbed over the fence that would be okay too.

The train was running as we talked, and though I couldn't hear it, I thought I would have to shout for the guy to hear me.
