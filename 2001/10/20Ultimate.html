11:15am CDT Saturday 20 October 2001

Fun at ultimate. We won 11 to 4 or 5 I think. The coolest thing was after the game, when Dave taught us a new throw! It's a forehand thrown behind the back. It's actually pretty easy once you know how to throw a forehand. Just got to get the butt out of the way. The coolest thing about this new throw is that before it's thrown, it looks like a forehand, but then *zing* the disc goes behind the thrower's back. Wow!

After the game, we played a 4 on 4 scrimmage to hold the field (which we had reserved) until the next teams showed up. That's when we learned the new throw. We were fending off a big group of soccer players who wanted the field. Of the next two teams, only one showed up, so they won and the soccer players got the field and everyone had a happy day.
