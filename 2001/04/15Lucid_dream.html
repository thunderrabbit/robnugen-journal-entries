7:41am CDT Sunday 15 April 2001

(lucid dream)

Being chased by two bad guys, I tried to fly away from them. I knew I should be able to fly, but I wasn't quite able to fly 100% anywhere I wanted to go. I could fly almost well. I flew slowly, as if the air were viscous, keeping me from getting the speed I needed to fly. I sank slowly toward the ground like a partially filled helium balloon.

But I needed to be able to fly! The bad guys were chasing me. Not like a "we're going to kill you" way, but in a harrassing way, like the bullies on Simpsons. I tried to use rocket/jet power through my feet to fly away, and tried to shoot them with machine gun cadence through my fists. Neither were effective against these guys.

So I used the biggest weapon I had against them. The one that always works, but gives me an unfair advantage over them. I woke up.
