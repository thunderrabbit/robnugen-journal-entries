## 19:53 Sunday 13 December 2015 JST

Nice time with Sarumama and Sarupapa this afternoon.  Lin made a lot
of food including nabe, and her signature egg pudding.  We (I) ate
chocolate covered raisins and some of the cakes they brought.

Topics ranged from Jennie to cats to houses to Jennie to the new
handles I made for our cabinet.
