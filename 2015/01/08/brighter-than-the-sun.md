##18:54 Wednesday 14 January 2015 JST##

(A few days ago,) near Kawasaki station, I noticed a reallly bright
spot in the middle of the intersection: sunlight reflected from a
nearby building.  It actually appeared that the reflected light was
more concentrated than regular sunlight; I guess the building is
slightly curved.
