2:05am CST Saturday 11 January 2003

Jackie came over, brought Josephine from her dad's house. Jack and I had rented movies, so we watched Death to Smoochy at Joe's recommendation. We thought it was pretty good and cute and bizarre. We all agreed that if we make a movie, we will have outtakes at the end. I was intrigued by the way the outtakes were also editted, not just pasted together in one long string.

After the movie the girls trimmed Jack's hair. Jackie considered buzzing *some* of her hair, just a little trim off the top, but decided against it. "I'm tempted," she kept saying.
