9:42pm CST Saturday 1 February 2003

A couple days ago I removed 3 names from my LJ friends list. Adrienne, Chez's cousin Paul, and Megan I think. Adrienne emailed me, asked me why I had removed her.

Fuck.

I told her it was because of the length of her entries, and though I encourage long journal entry writing, I don't have the patience/attention to read long entries. I think journals out to be written for ourselves, not written for others. (That's a big fat hard thing to do with my journal online, but I'm getting better at writing more of what I would write in my private book.)

Then I went to her journal and find that the recent 20 entries aren't all that long. Hmmm.

I read through the remains of my friends list and find that I wasn't really reading most of the entries. Only about 3 or 4 people's entries do I read. Maybe more. I seem to read the entries of people who I know well. That makes sense.

So, do I go slaughter more of my friends list? Alienate more people?

I've got too much fucking internet stuff on my daily plate already: emails, email lists, friends lists, writing my own journal, world news... argh fuck.

I'm on the edge of a cleaning mode, where I might just shred my LJ friends list or erase it altogether. But I know I won't erase it altogether.
