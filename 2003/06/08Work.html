Cause we've got tornadoes (and one of the students finished the word with me)........ I gave them the scary details of tornadoes (plus some vocab): chaotic, 300 mph winds, small, how they can disappate then regenerate, cause houses to explode (so we open windows to protect against that), etc. Two of the students seemed intrigued, and one wasn't all that talkative; I wonder if he'll tattle on me for a non Nova lesson.
