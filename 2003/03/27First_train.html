6:11pm JST Thursday 27 March 2003

On a train with Andrew (Dru Daddy G), Justin & Tara (married) who will be placed in Sendai.

We're being spoon fed train tickets and such for now. This ride is just over an hour it turns out. Assigned seats. I know the symbol for car (train car only?) now. It looks like the top view of a formula car with axels, but no wheels. Or a sideways tie fighter even though Andrew refuses to watch Star Wars.

6:48pm

I'm in Japan! I'm in Japan! I'm in Japan!!! How is it not really sinking in? Andrew was all, "yeah, no matter where I go, I wanna go somewhere else that sounds cooler," or something like that.

"But now you're stuck here for a year..."

Most amazing is how clean this place is! And people with colds wear little white masks like surgeons to keep from spreading germs. Almost like a fashion statement.
