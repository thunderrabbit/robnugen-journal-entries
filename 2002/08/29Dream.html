5:23am CDT Thursday 29 August 2002

(dream)

In an area with a shallow pool and I was surprised to see some cats sitting patiently at the bottom of the pool. I surmised it must be an optical illusion of some sort, but I got into the pool and it was no trick; there really were cats in there. I tried to avoid them as they seemed not too keen on having visitors, but one of them clawed me in the back of my right hand. I was able to get my hand away and then get out of the pool.
