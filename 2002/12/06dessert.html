5:01am PST Saturday 7 December 2002

Sally showed me where she used to live in Huntington Beach, basically midway between Newport Beach (her current digs) and LA. We had two cups of hot chocolate

("Would you like some more?"
"Yeah, gimme three.")

each, and split a pile of apple pancakes with banana and peanuts and whipped cream. Quite yummy.

Saw a house shaped like a medeival castle overlooking the beach, and saw where the real Navy Sals train and such. Awesome.

Saw fantastic waves coming to meet the shore along the asphalt path that winds along the beach for hours...
