1:01pm CDT Friday 26 April 2002

Just saw Gene as he returned my sleeping bag that he used for the MS 150. Checkin' out his fly bike, tri-density rubber tires, 16 spokes per wheel, alloy this, awesome that, when he mentioned that he'll be leaving Houston on Sunday.

I've known he was going to go, but this was the first time I realized that this was the last time I'll see him for a while. Such precious awesome memories of Wednesday Night Dinner at Dude's starring Gene Cuisine, with huge bowls of ice cream for dessert. Rolling around on bikes, three geeks with laptops on the couch, all wirelessly surfing on Dude's network. Gene's car getting bumped from behind on our way to New Year's Eve Party.

I'm thankful for everything.
