9:47pm CDT Thursday 27 June 2002

I just called Wende to see when she will finally get home; it seems like she's been gone forever!

She answered the phone, which was a huge surprise, and said she is in Houston at Taco Bell with Bob.

Bob? my brain thought about being jealous. "Who's Bob?"

"Jennifer's husband?" Wende's voice made a question mark at the end wondering why I didn't know who she was talking about.

"Oh, Bob."

In the background, I heard Wende say to Bob, "get a ton of hot sauce, don't forget a ton." Wende is so cute.

"Call me when you have a chance; I have some news for you," I said. She doesn't know that I plan to vacate the city soon.
