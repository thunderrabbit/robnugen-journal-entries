1:04am CST Saturday 9 November 2002

Funny things so far at Fall Conference:

  • During opening circle, we were asked to say one thing that we liked. I knew Josephine would pick pink. Laura or Jack (I think), said "the color, not the artist." I said, "I like Eminem, and M&M's." When it was Graham's turn, he said, "I like to think that people will have a good time this weekend." Brilliant!

  • Near the beginning of All My Friends and Neighbors, Erica Nagel didn't get to move for several rounds.

  • Edward refused to play All My Friends and Neighbors, hollering, "I'm disabled!!" His left thumb is severely bruised (he dropped a weight on it) and blue. The nail will likely come off soon. I suggested that he use a small drill (dremel) to drill a hole in the nail to release the pressure.

  • During the meet people game, where pairs of people introduced one another, I introduced Anthony, who's from here, wants to move to Italy and open a Judo Dojo, a restaurant where he will be the main chef, and a UU church. But actually he wants to play professional soccer. He has the best answer for what to wish for if a genie gives him one wish. And if he could have a super power, it would be to create fire of any temperature.

  • We never found out what that perfect wish would be.

  • I like the font on Kate's new tattoo "No one, not even the rain, has such small hands"

  • Amy and Amy introduced one another by signing their names and each saying "and she has the best name ever!"

  • There was certainly other funny stuff, but I've forgotten. Good night, all.
