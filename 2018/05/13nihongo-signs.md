Thanks to [jisho.org radical search](https://jisho.org/#radical), I was able to find most of the kanji.  I used the [handwriting search](https://jisho.org/#handwriting) for this one: 断 (pronounced だん (like the given name Don) transliterated as "dan"), which means decision.

So here is a transcription of the sign:

    防犯上の理由より
    ０時３０分〜６時３０分までの間
    イートインスペースの使用は
    中断させていただきます

And my translation:

    To reduce crime, please don't use
    the eat-in space between 12:30am and 6:30am.

And [Google's translation](https://translate.google.com/#auto/en/%E9%98%B2%E7%8A%AF%E4%B8%8A%E3%81%AE%E7%90%86%E7%94%B1%E3%82%88%E3%82%8A%0A%EF%BC%90%E6%99%82%EF%BC%93%EF%BC%90%E5%88%86%E3%80%9C%EF%BC%96%E6%99%82%EF%BC%93%EF%BC%90%E5%88%86%E3%81%BE%E3%81%A7%E3%81%AE%E9%96%93%0A%E3%82%A4%E3%83%BC%E3%83%88%E3%82%A4%E3%83%B3%E3%82%B9%E3%83%9A%E3%83%BC%E3%82%B9%E3%81%AE%E4%BD%BF%E7%94%A8%E3%81%AF%0A%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%AD%E3%81%95%E3%81%9B%E3%81%A6%E3%81%84%E3%81%9F%E3%81%A0%E3%81%8D%E3%81%BE%E3%81%99) (as of this post date, May 13th, 2018 (Mother's Day, btw))
