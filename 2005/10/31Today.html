feeding apples to a horse
picking olives that were big and fat and plentiful
watching the ordinance explode, but not cause the grenade to blow
being herded behind the truck for the explosion
being told we could not watch the explosion
being told we could not record the event
being told we could put our  cameras on the front of the truck

Chris told us a cool story of his adventures in Cairo while he learned Arabic for two months at a school. I want to do that; no more learning languages by teaching Japanese. Just pay the $1000 for tuition , housing and food and hook up the learnin'.
