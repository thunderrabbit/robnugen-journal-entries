##16:09 Monday 15 December 2014 JST##

Actually, it's not really a maze; there are no dead ends except for
the start and finish points.  It's actually a puzzle to figure out how
to connect the start and end points.

Anyway, After about 4 hours of work, I've finished the final draft:

[![black path snaking around under green and pink lines](/images/2014/12/thumbs/final draft.png)](/images/2014/12/final draft.png)

Now I just need to draw all the black lines on the actual panels.

##21:00 Monday 15 December 2014 JST##
