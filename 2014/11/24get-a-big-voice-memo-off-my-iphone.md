##11:11 Monday 24 November 2014 JST##

Visit [http://www.macroplant.com/iexplorer/](http://www.macroplant.com/iexplorer/)

Click Download

![download iexplorer](/images/2014/11/download iexplorer.png)

Open the .dmg file

![open .dmg file](/images/2014/11/open dmg file.png)

Click, drag, and drop the left icon onto the right one.

![install iExplorer](/images/2014/11/install iExplorer.png)

Click continue with demo

![continue with demo](/images/2014/11/click continue with demo.png)

Click voice memos

![click voice memos](/images/2014/11/click voice memos.png)

Select the memo(s) you want to download

![select the memo to download](/images/2014/11/select the memos you want to download.png)

click [Export seleced item(s) to folder...]

![export items to folder](/images/2014/11/export items to folder.png)

Download the file somewhere

![download to a folder](/images/2014/11/download to a folder.png)

The file will have a green checkmark once it has been transferred.

![see a green check](/images/2014/11/green checkmark.png)
