## 21:56 Friday 01 January 2016 JST

So tired.  We hiked 16 kilometers, ascending (and descending) the 1610
meter 滝子山, Mt Takigo, though I don't know from what elevation we
started.  Great to chill (haha) with Travis and Yumi!
