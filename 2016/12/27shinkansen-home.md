## 14:37 Tuesday 25 December 2016 JST

(written 10:37 Tuesday 27 December 2016 JST)

We took the bullet train home.  So much faster than the bus!  No
electrical outlet on this particular train though, so I didn't write
anything on my computer.  But I did take a ton of pictures, not all of
which I will post here.

Or should I?  Hmmmmm.

## 10:57

Okay I pulled them all down from my camera (via
https://photos.google.com), and I'm pushing them up to
https://b.robnugen.com now via
[Mountain Duck](https://mountainduck.io/), which gives an interface
into Finder for drag and drop.  I kinda think it will fail and I'll
have to whip out the command line and actually copy and paste some
directory names.

## 11:35
